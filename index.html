Hey! I’m Robert.

Currently bouldering V4s at Central Rock Gyms in Boston, MA.

Occupationally, I'm learning (and machine learning) at Avronna, where I learned to tell stories.

I go to Vanderbilt and am pursuing a B.S./M.S. in Computer Science, with a second major in Applied Mathematics. You can find some of the cool classes that I've taken here.

I'm also currently building Limelight, am an executive board member at VINES, security at Instagram, growth at Coursera, infrastructure at Cadre, engineering effectiveness at Improbable, and server performance at Microsoft. I also did field research to stop elephants 🐘 from running over farm fields at the MIT D-Lab in Seronga, Botswana.

I was also previously a Kleiner Perkins Engineering Fellow, a Rough Draft Academy Scholar, and a University Innovation Fellow at the Stanford d.school.

I spend my pastime working on my motorcycle 🏍, reading about history 📚, mentoring fellow first-gen low-income students in tech 🎓, and playing the guitar 🎸. Sometimes I dabble with streetwear and obsess over vaporwave and the 90s.

Come say hi— I'm always looking for a ski or hike buddy!
